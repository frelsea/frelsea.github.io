Our Purpose

We are a boutique technical consultancy bringing "Oil Major standards" to the independent shipowner. Small fleets often lack the leverage or technical depth to challenge shipyards, suppliers, and managers effectively. Frelsea bridges that gap. We act as your independent technical auditor, using data and experience to strip out waste, ensure regulatory compliance, and verify that your vessels are being managed cost-effectively.
