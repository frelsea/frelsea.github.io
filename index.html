Our Purpose

We are maritime experts with extensive experience in ship management, repair and modernisation projects, new build projects and more. In our professional lives, we encountered problems that required a lot of time, and resources to solve. At Frelsea, we share this knowledge and experience with our clients.

what we do

Ship owners and operators can focus on their business as we handle the technical challenges they may face.

Ship operators and owners have a lot on their plate. From ensuring vessels are seaworthy and compliant, to cargo operations and scheduling, to dealing with customers and suppliers - the list goes on. That's where Frelsea comes in. We're a maritime consultancy that uses our experience, knowledge and expertise to solve any problem ship owners or operators may face. Here are some examples of our work scope:

  • Prepurchase inspections
  • Damage surveys
  • Prevetting inspections & consulting
  • Procedure & manual writing
  • Auditing
  • Drydock preparation & execution
  • Parts & service supply
  • Shipyard selection
  • Temporary superintendents
  • Design & engineering
