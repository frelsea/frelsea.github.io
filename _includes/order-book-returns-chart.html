The order book and the return that followed
Industry investment against the realised one-year return on a five-year-old Panamax. Source: Greenwood and Hanson (2015), from Clarksons monthly data, 1976 to 2011.
The marked points and the return extremes are figures reported in the study. The sloping line shows the documented negative relationship between industry investment, measured as deliveries net of demolitions, and subsequent returns. High demolition activity carries the opposite sign and precedes high returns.
