Who We Are

Technical Directors on Demand.

Frelsea was founded to bridge the gap between "Small Fleet" budgets and "Oil Major" standards.

We observed that independent shipowners and investors often lack the in-house technical depth to effectively challenge shipyards, vetting inspectors, or third-party managers. This leads to inflated OpEx, poor repair quality, and regulatory risks.

Frelsea is not a generalist ship manager. We are a boutique technical consultancy led by a Senior Technical Director with deep experience in the tanker and gas sectors. We provide the high-level oversight usually reserved for corporate fleets, tailored for the independent owner.

Our Philosophy

Technical Asset Protection.

We believe that "trust but verify" is the only way to manage maritime assets.

Our model is built on Technical Asset Protection. We don't just facilitate operations; we audit them. We leverage a global network of trusted independent surveyors, naval architects, and former superintendents to execute inspections on the ground, while our core leadership provides the strategic analysis and decision-making support.

Whether it's auditing a drydock specification to prevent scope creep or analyzing EU ETS exposure, we act as the shipowner's technical firewall.
