about us

Expertise. Network. Results.

Frelsea is a boutique maritime consultancy offering expertise in ship management, technical operations, corporate structuring, and strategic advisory for shipowners. We operate through a core leadership backed by a network of trusted professionals in shipbuilding, engineering, naval architecture, and finance, enabling us to deliver tailored solutions across disciplines.

what we do

Technical, Commercial & Strategic Advisory

We support shipowners in navigating technical, commercial, and strategic challenges. Our services cover pre-purchase inspections, technical consulting, drydock and refit project management, corporate structuring, capital strategy, fleet acquisitions, and investment advisory. Whether optimizing existing operations or planning for growth, we provide solutions designed around your objectives.
