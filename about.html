about us

A multi-national team of experts.

With more than a century in combined experience, our maritime experts help shipowners and managers achieve excellence in their operations and handle challenges with easy precision. Our multi-national team consists of professional with backgrounds in shipbuilding, ship-repair, engineering, naval architecture, classification societies, ship management and more.

what we do

technical & commercial

Our main client base consists of ship-owners who find themselves overwhelmed with technical and commercial challenges common in the shipping industry. Depending on the customer's need, we provide either consulting services (remote or physical) as well as turn-key solutions. This can be as simple as a pre-purchase inspection or it can be project management for a major refit.
